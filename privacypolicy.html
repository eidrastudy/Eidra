Privacy Policy

Eidra respects your privacy and only collects minimal information required to provide the service.

Information Collected

Eidra may collect the following information when users download study materials:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Mobile number
  • Academic details (degree, semester)
  • Selected book

Purpose of Data

This information is used solely for:

  • Improving the platform and recommending relevant resources
  • Understanding usage patterns to enhance our service
  • Providing better, more targeted academic materials

Data Storage

Information is stored securely using Google Sheets with restricted access. Eidra does not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We take reasonable measures to protect your information.

Third-Party Services

Eidra may use third-party tools such as analytics platforms (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand site traffic and usage. These services may collect anonymous browsing data. Additionally, our book downloads are currently simulated, but future integrations may involve trusted providers.

User Consent

By using the Eidra platform, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using our services.
